Every Man For Himself!

How much of a blank slate should a new site/app be?

  • The leaner you start the more you have to build.
  • Look at open source, we always start from something that exists.
  • Imagine if we always started from zero to something.

Frameworks, scaffolding, CMS, bootstrap, etc...

These are all starting points that push us into the future and save us time.

What about Standards?

Standards are a kind of Framework, they give us the same benefits (time). But...

  • Unlike Frameworks, they touch every bit of code
  • They make many sound like one
  • They offer an easy door for every newcomer

So Standards are good...

In fact great...

Hell, Mandatory!

And now what?

  • Where do I start?
  • Which is the way to go?

You make your own way!

My advice is...

  • There is no one way of having/making Standards
  • You can start from scratch (cof... cof...)
  • You should start from the bountiful resources
  • Make the first step, it's not like it'll be set in stone
  • Involve you team (if you have one)
  • Share your rule set
  • Make mistakes and...

Evolve!
